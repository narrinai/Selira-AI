18 U.S.C. 2257 Exemption Statement

Last updated: December 2025

Exemption Notice

Selira AI is exempt from the record-keeping requirements of 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and 28 C.F.R. Part 75 because all visual content displayed on this website is entirely computer-generated using artificial intelligence.

All characters depicted on Selira AI are 100% AI-generated. No real human beings, models, or performers are depicted in any content on this platform.

AI-Generated Content Statement

All visual depictions of individuals appearing on this website are created entirely through artificial intelligence image generation technology, including but not limited to:

  • Character avatars and profile images
  • User-generated companion images
  • All visual content within the platform

These images are synthetic creations produced by AI models (such as Stable Diffusion and Flux) and do not depict any actual persons, whether living or deceased.

Age Representation

All AI-generated characters on Selira AI are designed and represented as adults aged 18 years or older. Our content moderation systems actively prevent the creation or display of any characters that could be interpreted as minors.

Users must verify they are 18 years of age or older before accessing the platform.

No User Uploads

Selira AI does not permit users to upload photographs or images of real individuals. All visual content is generated through our AI systems based on text-based prompts and predefined character parameters.

Legal Basis for Exemption

Under 18 U.S.C. § 2257, record-keeping requirements apply to producers of actual sexually explicit content depicting real individuals. Since Selira AI exclusively features AI-generated imagery with no real human subjects, these requirements do not apply to our platform.

This exemption is consistent with the statutory language and regulatory guidance, which focus on the protection of real individuals who participate in the creation of adult content.
