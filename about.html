GDPR Compliance

Respecting your rights under European data protection law

Selira AI is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the world's strongest data protection law. We respect and uphold all your rights as a user.

Your GDPR Rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you at any time
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate personal data we may have
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Restrict how we process your data

Data We Collect: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service: email address for account creation, chat history (encrypted), and usage statistics (anonymized).

Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing and improving our service. You can withdraw consent at any time.
