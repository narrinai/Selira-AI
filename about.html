GDPR Compliance

Respecting your rights under European data protection law

Selira AI is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the world's strongest data protection law. We respect and uphold all your rights as a user.

Your GDPR Rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you at any time
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate personal data we may have
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Restrict how we process your data

Data We Collect: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service: email address for account creation, chat history (encrypted), and usage statistics (anonymized).

Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent and our legitimate interest in providing and improving our service. You can withdraw consent at any time.

Data Protection Officer: For any GDPR-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@selira.ai

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Selira AI really free?
Yes! Selira AI is completely free with unlimited chats and 1 free image generation credit daily. We also offer a Pro subscription with additional features like unlimited image generation, priority access, and exclusive characters, but free unlimited chat remains available to everyone forever.
How do you protect my privacy?
We use 256-bit encryption for all conversations, host our servers in secure EU data centers, and maintain a strict zero data retention policy. We never read your messages, never sell your data, and give you complete control to delete everything anytime.
Can I really delete my data?
Yes. You can request account deletion from your profile settings, which permanently removes all your data—chats, images, and account information. We process deletion requests promptly and don't keep backups of deleted data.
What kind of AI companions are available?
We have over 1,000 unique AI companions in both realistic and anime styles. Choose from female and male companions across various personalities, themes, and scenarios. You can also create your own custom companions with our companion creator.
Are there any content restrictions?
Selira AI is an 18+ platform designed for adults. We support both SFW and NSFW content, with clear filtering options. All users must verify they are 18 or older to use the platform.
How does the image generator work?
Our AI image generator uses advanced models to create custom images based on your prompts. Simply describe what you want, select your preferences (realistic or anime style, gender, etc.), and generate images. Free users get 1 credit daily, while Pro subscribers enjoy unlimited generation.
Is my chat history saved?
Your chat history is saved encrypted so you can continue conversations later, but only you can access it. You can delete any chat or your entire history anytime. We never access or read your conversations.
Do you use my data to train AI models?
No. We never use your private conversations to train AI models. Your chats are encrypted and private to you. We don't sell, share, or use your data for any purpose beyond providing the service to you.
What payment methods do you accept for Pro?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods through Stripe. All payments are processed securely and we never store your payment information.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime from your profile settings. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, you'll return to the free plan with unlimited chat.
How do I create my own companion?
Click "Create" in the sidebar, then customize everything: name, appearance, personality, greeting message, and avatar. You can make your companion public for others to chat with or keep it private for your use only.
Is Selira AI available on mobile?
Yes! Selira AI is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices through your web browser. Simply visit selira.ai on your phone or tablet for the full experience.
How can I contact support?
Visit our Contact page to reach our support team. We typically respond within 24 hours to all inquiries.
